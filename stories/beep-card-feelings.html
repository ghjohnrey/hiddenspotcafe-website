The Beep Card That Charged Based on Your Feelings

Short Taglish fantasy read for commuters.

⏱️ 2 min read #mrt #urban #taglish

Lia noticed it on a random Tuesday.

Nag-tap siya sa MRT.

Instead of ₱15, the screen showed:

₱0.00

“Libre?” she whispered.

The guard just waved her through.

Later that day, she found out she passed her qualifying exam.

Coincidence?

The next morning, she tapped again.

₱35.00

“Ha? Bakit ang mahal?”

That afternoon, she received an email.

We regret to inform you…

She didn’t get the internship.

Then one Friday, it flashed:

₱0.00

Minsan, advance payment siya for relief.
